Sales Gallery Tool

100E53

Product Designers: Yoonjo Choi
Duration: 2016
Tools: Sketch, OmniGraffle

Overview

100E53 is a luxury high-rise residential building in Midtown Manhattan, New York. Building was under development while the project was in process. A project of Foster and Partners – DBOX engaged in CGI, Branding & Design and Digital products of the Building and Sales gallery.

The Sales Gallery Tool is a Web Application customized for an iPad. This tool serves as a remote control a Broker can use while leading a potential Buyer. The remote controls the display screens and 3D models of the building throughout the sales gallery.

My role for this project was to collect user and business needs and take lead of specs and experience. Documentation included sitemaps, functional specs, flowcharts, use cases, low-fi wireframes and prototypes.
