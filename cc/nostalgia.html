Yoonjo Choi

Nostalgia

Interactive Artwork
Typewriter(Readymade)
2011

Nostalgia is an interactive art piece where you can view a memory of my past by typing. Typing in triggers a sensor which brightens a photo that is projected on the paper of the typewriter.The photo will fade away if typing is stopped, as if memories will if you do not hold on to them. The spacebar and carriage return on the left side can also be used. Each have a different function. The spacebar lets you flip to the next photo and the handle on the left side, which is the Enter key, lets you jump to another time period of my life.
